Product Manager, SaaS Growth (AI/ML Focus)

About Us:
We are a fast-growing SaaS startup specializing in AI-driven solutions for business intelligence. Our mission is to empower companies with actionable insights derived from complex data.

Role Overview:
We are seeking an experienced and innovative Product Manager to lead the development and growth of our new AI/ML-powered analytics platform. You will be responsible for the entire product lifecycle, from ideation to launch and post-launch optimization. This role requires a strong understanding of machine learning concepts, data analytics, and user-centric design principles.

Key Responsibilities:
- Define and articulate product vision, strategy, and roadmap for AI/ML features.
- Conduct market research, competitive analysis, and user research to identify opportunities.
- Translate complex technical concepts into clear, concise product requirements and user stories.
- Collaborate closely with engineering, data science, design, and sales teams.
- Manage the product backlog, prioritize features, and oversee sprint planning.
- Monitor product performance, analyze usage data, and iterate based on insights.
- Drive Go-To-Market strategy and enable sales and marketing teams.

Required Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Business, or a related field.
- 5+ years of product management experience, with at least 2 years in AI/ML or data-intensive SaaS products.
- Proven experience with agile development methodologies (Scrum/Kanban).
- Strong analytical skills and experience with data-driven decision making.
- Excellent communication, presentation, and interpersonal skills.
- Familiarity with AI/ML concepts (e.g., NLP, predictive modeling, deep learning).

Preferred Qualifications:
- MBA or Master's degree in a technical field.
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP).
- Hands-on experience with SQL, Python, or R for data analysis.
- Prior startup experience.
